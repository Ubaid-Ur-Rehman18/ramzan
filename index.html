Ramzan Mubarak 2025

اللهم اجعلنا من عتقاء شهر رمضان واغفر لنا ذنوبنا وتقبل صيامنا وقيامنا

"O Allah, make us among those who are freed from the Hellfire in this blessed month of Ramadan, forgive our sins, and accept our fasting and prayers."

May this holy month bring peace and prosperity!
